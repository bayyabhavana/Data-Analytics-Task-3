# 📊 Task 3 – Deep Dive Analysis & Interactive Dashboarding

## 🎯 Objective
To define key business KPIs, perform deep-dive analysis, and build an interactive dashboard to support business decision-making.

---

## 📌 KPIs Defined

- Total Revenue
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Total Customers
- Revenue by Product
- Revenue by Age Group

---

## 🔎 Deep-Dive Analysis Performed

Customer Segmentation Analysis based on:

- Age Group
- Revenue Contribution
- Purchase Behavior

---

## 📈 Key Findings

- Adult customers contribute higher total revenue.
- Young customers represent majority of transactions.
- Laptop is the top revenue-generating product.
- Average Order Value indicates moderate spending pattern.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Power BI (for dashboard)

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

The interactive dashboard includes:

- KPI Cards (Revenue, AOV, Customers)
- Revenue by Product (Bar Chart)
- Revenue by Age Group
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Revenue by Gender

---

## ✅ Outcome

The deep-dive analysis enables better customer targeting, product strategy, and business growth decisions.
