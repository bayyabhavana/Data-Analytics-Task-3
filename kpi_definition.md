# 📊 KPI Definitions – Task 3

## 1️⃣ Total Revenue
Formula:
SUM(Purchase_Amount)

Purpose:
Measures total business sales performance.

---

## 2️⃣ Average Order Value (AOV)
Formula:
Total Revenue / Total Transactions

Purpose:
Measures average spending per transaction.

---

## 3️⃣ Total Customers
Formula:
COUNT(DISTINCT Customer_ID)

Purpose:
Identifies total unique customers.

---

## 4️⃣ Revenue by Product
Formula:
SUM(Purchase_Amount) GROUP BY Product

Purpose:
Identifies best-performing products.

---

## 5️⃣ Revenue by Age Group
Formula:
SUM(Purchase_Amount) GROUP BY Age_Group

Purpose:
Supports customer segmentation strategy.
