# 📊 Deep Dive Analysis Report – Task 3

## 📌 Objective
To perform customer segmentation analysis and identify high-value customer groups.

---

## 🔎 Analysis Performed

Customer Segmentation based on Age Group:
- Young (<30)
- Adult (≥30)

Revenue contribution and purchase behavior were analyzed.

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Adult customers generate higher total revenue.
- Young customers form the majority customer base.
- Laptop contributes the highest product revenue.
- Average Order Value shows stable purchasing behavior.

---

## 📊 Business Implications

- Focus marketing campaigns on Adult segment.
- Offer promotions targeting Young customers.
- Invest more in top-performing product categories.

---

## ✅ Conclusion

Deep-dive analysis provides actionable insights for:
- Customer targeting
- Revenue optimization
- Business growth strategy
